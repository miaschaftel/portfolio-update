ABOUT

I am a junior studying User Experience (UX) Design in the School of Information and on the Cappo Sales Track through the Ross Business School at the University of Michigan. Through coursework I am experienced with Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL, and I am currently studying front-end programming for web design using the POUR design principles of accessible design. I am also familiar with Sketch, InVision, Figma, and other industry-standard design tools.


On campus I'm involved in organizations such as Michigan Business Women, the Ross Retail Club, the Ross Sales Club, and Alpha Phi International Fraternity.

I am passionate about creating products and experiences that are transformative and accessible for users. My past experiences in UX/UI Design, Sales Planning, and Product and Business Development provide me with a distinct set of strengths and skills which allow me to have a unique perspective in the product development and user experience design process.
