HappyLiving

A platform for students in college to connect and get advice related to their living experiences.

Timeline:

4 1/2 weeks, Sep - Oct 2021

Roles:

Digital Product Designer, Product Manager

Skills:

UX/UI Design, Graphic Design, Sketching, Empathy Mapping, User Personas, Wireframing, Prototyping, Usability Testing

Tools:

Figma, Procreate, Pivotal Tracker

Description:

I came up with the idea for HappyLiving after seeing friends struggle to adjust to college life and living with a roommate. The product idea came naturally and felt like the best solution for a college-aged market given the popularlity of social media products within the age group. I sketched out my ideas and developed them into wireframes in Figma. I designed the high-fidelity prototype in Figma which is linked to the image above and displayed below.

Process:

I began development by sketching out ideas for the interface. My main focus was creating a platform centered around building a community: to share and connect. These sketches show the early ideas I then adapted into wireframes.

Wireframes:

From the sketches I began to narrow down the key functions and features. I developed a clear image for the product and began wireframing in Figma.
