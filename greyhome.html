Greyhome Partners

Developing a refreshed look and feel using Webflow to implement CSS and HTML into a more advanced and responsive site.

Timeline:

3 weeks, June 2020 (Initial Design)

8 weeks, October 2021 - Present (Active)

Roles:

Lead Designer, Product Manager

Skills:

UX/UI Design, Graphic Design, Sketching, HTML, CSS

Tools:

Squarespace, Webflow, Procreate

Description:

My work with Greyhome Partners first started in June 2020 when I developed and designed their first website using Squarespace. The process took 3 weeks. The end product was a simple but complete website that had the bare minimum but necessary information. At this point in time many of the company's projects had yet to be completed so the website represented the early stages in the company's development.

Throughout my time working with the company I made gradual improvements and changes to the initial website, but no major changes had been made since 2020. Apart from this, though, I have been working to create a new image for the company. I modified the original logo to create a sleeker graphic with fewer colors. I did some work on their Instagram but the majority of my work is related to their website and graphic designs.

In October 2021 the company reached out asking me to upload lots of new content to the existing website. I found working with Squarespace was not the best experience and after taking new classes through the School of Information I learned about better alternatives. I decided to move to Webflow where I have better control over the layout and design since the platform uses CSS and HTML. The design and development process through Webflow has been very smooth. I am producing a product I am much more supportive of and confident in. The website is nearly finished and I am excited to display the final product soon.
